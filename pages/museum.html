Experience in Future Museum

This project aims to explore interactive 3D experience for future museum. Cutting-edge technologies like AR (Augmented Reality), VR ( Virtual Reality), and MR ( Mixed Reality) are changing our way of seeing and interacting with the real world. In this project, I used Danzer Tiling (3D space filling method) to design for future museum. On the physical side, I researched in to screens, projections, and space syntax. On the virtual side, I explored how to implement interactive systems using VR and AR. Going beyond these, these techniques and experiences plays with users' feelings and create the hyperreality prototype.
